Coalition of Nigerian Youths for Peter Obi

ABOUT US

The Coalition of Nigerian Youths for Peter Obi (CONYPO) is a support group founded with the aim of mobilizing votes for the Labour Party's presidential candidate, Mr Peter Obi. Elections are not won online and we aim to achieve this through intense campaigning and sensitization of the electorates to even the most remote parts of the country. There is hope and together we can build a better Nigeria by electing a credible and competent leader come 2023.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is simply to mobilize votes for the people's choice, Mr Peter Obi and also to sensitize the electorates on the need to get it right in 2023.

We have to get it right in the next elections and Peter Obi is the man Nigeria needs! -

Chukwuebuka Ukandu,

Convener CONYPO.

The CONYPO Fund

The Coalition of Nigerian Youths for Peter Obi fund is used to mobilize and facilitate all campaign activities of the group across all states of the federation and the FCT. This includes organizing a 3 day nationwide awareness and sensitization rally, printing of branded materials (t-shirts, fliers, flags, placards, banners etc), sponsoring of radio, TV and social media ads as well as helicopter parades in select cities. We have a target to raise N1 billion naira for this project and we are calling on every young Nigerian who supports Mr Peter Obi both at home and in the diaspora to please donate a minimum of N1000 to the fund to enable us achieve our goals and objectives.

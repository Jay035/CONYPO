Coalition of Nigerian Youths for Peter Obi

ABOUT US

The Coalition of Nigerian Youths for Peter Obi (CONYPO) is a support group founded with the aim of mobilizing votes for the Labour Party's presidential candidate, Mr Peter Obi. Elections are not won online and we aim to achieve this through intense campaigning and sensitization of the electorates to even the most remote parts of the country. There is hope and together we can build a better Nigeria by electing a credible and competent leader come 2023.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is simply to help mobilize votes for the people's choice, Mr Peter Obi and also to sensitize the electorate on the need to get it right in 2023. We aim to achieve this through strategic awareness rallys across Nigeria and grassroots sensitization outreaches.

I'm calling on all young supporters of Mr Peter Obi both in Nigeria and the diaspora to join us in the fight for a better Nigeria. We need a competent and vibrant leader come 2023 and Mr Peter Obi is the best candidate for the job. Through our collective efforts, we will make our voices heard and our votes count while supporting him with the structure he needs to win at the polls. -

Chukwuebuka,

Convener CONYPO.
